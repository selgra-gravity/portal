Join SELGRA at Florence 2026

SELGRA Day — inaugural edition

3 August 2026 · 9:00–16:00 · Florence, Italy

The first-ever SELGRA Day takes place on the opening day of the COSPAR/ELGRA symposium at Fortezza da Basso. Free and open to all students interested in gravitational and space research, whether or not you are a SELGRA member.

Morning

Walking tour, gelato, and caffè around Florence

Afternoon

Interactive session at the University of Florence

Evening

Drinks at a local wine bar

This is the community event of the year. Come join us, meet fellow students and researchers from across Europe, and build friendships that will carry through your whole career in gravity research. We can not wait to welcome you to Florence.

About SELGRA

Mission

SELGRA supports students and early-career researchers who want to learn, collaborate and grow within the European low gravity research community.

What we do

We host educational formats, research discussions and professional development activities for the low gravity research community.

Who it is for

Researchers, educators, clinicians, engineers, designers, data scientists and students who want to work across space, health and education.
